About My College

Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT) IIIT Ongole, established in 2016, is part of Andhra Pradesh's initiative to enhance the educational framework by integrating teaching and research. Situated in Ongole, the institute focuses primarily on technology and offers a unique six-year integrated B.Tech program starting after the 10th standard. The curriculum is designed to provide a profound understanding of engineering along with a strong base in sciences and humanities, preparing students comprehensively for technological challenges. Despite being relatively new, RGUKT IIIT Ongole has quickly gained recognition for its focus on holistic education and its emphasis on practical, hands-on experiences that equip students for both academic and industry challenges. The campus embodies a vibrant academic atmosphere with state-of-the-art facilities, fostering innovation and research .This institution aims to produce technically proficient graduates who are ready to contribute to the fast-paced technological advancements globally..
